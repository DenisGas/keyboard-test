How to Test Your Keyboard

Use this virtual keyboard to test your physical keyboard. Press any key to see if it works correctly. The virtual keyboard will highlight the keys as you press them.

The keyboard is the primary tool for entering text on your computer or laptop. However, issues like sticky keys, incorrect symbols, or unresponsive keys can occur over time. This online tool helps you test your keyboard to determine if the problem lies in the hardware or software.

Simply press the keys on your physical keyboard. If a key works correctly, it will light up in gray while pressed and turn yellow after you release it on the virtual keyboard. If a key sticks or doesn’t respond, you’ll notice it doesn’t highlight as expected.

This online keyboard tester is a simple and convenient way to check your keyboard without downloading any software. It works directly in your browser, making it accessible anytime.

Testing your keyboard is straightforward but requires attention. Press each key to ensure it functions properly. If a key doesn’t work as expected, you may need to clean your keyboard, replace it, or check for software issues.

Mechanical keyboards remain the most popular choice for typing due to their tactile feedback and durability. However, they can still experience issues like double-typing, false presses, or stuck keys. Use this tool to identify and troubleshoot these problems.
